Smart-Technology Enabled Classrooms

Smart-Technology Enabled Classrooms

Smart classes – The effective education technologies are used to give an altogether new and exciting experience to learn through watching and learning the subjects with the help of videos of the contents of the syllabus.

Class Rooms

Class Rooms

SKV Vidhyaashram has well organised infrastructure facilities. Mainly it has beautifully designed A/C White digi-board classroom and imported furniture. The colorful building with beautiful landscaping gives a happy and energetic feel to the students.

Faculty 

Faculty

SKV believes in the notion that teachers should play an important role in the life of a student. SKVV faculty maintain a good rapport with the students to nurture the spirit and bring out the hidden talent in every student. Faculties are well trained, well experienced and dedicated.

Laboratory

Laboratory

Our school has excellent laboratory facilities with all necessary equipments which allow students to gain sound practical knowledge and hands on experience. Functional computer and science labs are available for the students where they can learn, explore, quest, hypothesise and discover the joy of mechanics of normal practical life. Our laboratories are well-equipped to meet the requirements of CBSE syllabus and also provide access to experimentation and exploration of young minds.

Library

Library

"A man only learns in two ways, one by reading and the other by association with smarter people".

A palatial accommodation is provided to our school library books. The library is an array of very good subject oriented reference books with intensive and extensive academic collection. Kids section is filled with cartoon CD, Rhymes CD and kids book. Reference section has a variety of standard reference books on almost all topics at the school level.

Club Activities and Languages

Club Activities and Languages

The school offers students a wide range of club activities appropriate to their age. Joining a club is one of the many ways through which students can be encouraged to enhance their skill set. In order to develop the talent of our students and to nurture their interests and aptitudes, we have various clubs and inter-home activities in various field such as literary, performing arts, photographic club, Eco club, Math Club, Science Club, GK Club, IT Club, Philately Club, Quiz etc., These activities help to promote team spirit and healthy competition among our students.

Career Counselling For Senior and Secondary Students

Career Counselling For Senior and Secondary Students

We at SKV Vidhyaashram take a proactive role in ensuring that our students make wise career choices. To enable them to achieve this, we invite professionals from various walks of life who explain our secondary students the nuances of their profession - the institutions and courses that lead to their careers, the opportunities and challenges involved and so on. Our students find these interactive sessions extremely useful.

Hostel

Hostel

We are committed to provide modern infrastructure with hygienic and sanitised living conditions for all. We ensure happy and creative environment and inculcate social etiquette. We take care to ensure that the food is of high quality and that it contains all the necessary nourishment for healthy growing bodies and minds.

  • Separate hostel for boys & girls
  • A/C & non A/C rooms & dorm
  • After school hours, study & doubt clearing session under the guidance of subject experts
  • Parental care • Experienced & trained warden
  • In-house medical facilities
  • Triple menu
  • Both veg and non-veg dishes and snacks
  • Sports and other recreational activities
Transport

Transport

Safety of the students and a comfortable journey to and fro are motivating factors of our excellent transport department.

  • Safe and well-networked bus facility.
  • Global Positioning Routing System (GPRS) enabled tracking system.
  • For Day Scholars - School Bus pick-up and drop for in & around 30 kms radius.
  • For Hostel students - during Holidays – School Bus drop and pick - up for all districts.
